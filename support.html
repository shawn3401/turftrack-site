Common questions

How do I reset my password?

On the login screen, tap "Sign in with password instead," then tap "Forgot password?" and follow the instructions sent to your email.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iPhone to cancel or manage your TurfTrack subscription.

My data disappeared after logging in on a new device.

Make sure you're signing in with the same email address you used when you created your account. Each account's data is tied to that login.

Can I use TurfTrack on multiple properties?

TurfTrack is designed for a single property with multiple zones. Multi-property support is on our roadmap for a future update.
