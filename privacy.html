Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

Overview

TurfTrack ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: Your email address and password, collected when you create an account
  • Yard and lawn data: Information you enter about your yards, including size, grass type, fertilizer applications, treatments, tasks, and journal entries
  • Support communications: Messages you send through the in-app support chat

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the TurfTrack app and your account
  • To store and sync your lawn care data across sessions
  • To respond to support requests
  • To send transactional emails related to your account or support escalations

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Third-Party Services

TurfTrack uses the following third-party services:

  • Clerk — for authentication and account management
  • Supabase — for data storage
  • Resend — for support-related email delivery

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at support@turftrack.ai.

Children's Privacy

TurfTrack is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notice.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at support@turftrack.ai.
